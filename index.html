About Me

Hello! I'm a data science student who likes turning complex data into meaningful insights. Currently pursuing my Master’s in Data Science at the University of North Texas, I specialize in Database Management Systems, Data Visualization, Machine Learning and Web Scraping.

I have a strong academic background with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Data Analytics from Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), India. Throughout my journey, I've combined technical expertise with creative problem-solving to deliver impactful projects in healthcare and retail analytics.

I believe data is not just numbers — it tells a story, drives innovation, and solves real-world problems. My goal is to harness data to empower smarter decisions, drive meaningful change, and make technology more human.

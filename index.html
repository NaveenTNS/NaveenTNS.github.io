About Me

Hello! I’m Naveen Sai Tamanampudi, a data science professional with a strong foundation in Analytics, Machine learning, and Artificial Intelligence. I am pursuing my Master’s in Data Science at the University of North Texas, specializing in Database Management Systems, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Generative AI, and Data Visualization.

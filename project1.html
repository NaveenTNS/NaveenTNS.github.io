Adidas Performance Analysis (Power BI)

In this project, I transformed Adidas sales data into actionable insights using Power BI, revealing key trends and areas for improvement. My analysis highlighted top-performing products and regions, identified inefficiencies in inventory, and ultimately empowered data-driven decision-making. This work demonstrates my ability to translate complex data into strategic recommendations that drive growth and profitability.

Key Insights:
• Analysis showed that Men’s Street Footwear is the top-selling product category, generating $27.6 million in sales. This insight highlights where Adidas should focus marketing and inventory efforts to maximize revenue.
• New York led all cities in total sales ($8.7 million), while Tennessee recorded the highest operating margin (50.7%). These differences suggest that tailoring strategies by region can improve profitability and market penetration.
• Some products consistently underperformed, leading to excess inventory and forced discounting. This underscores the need for strategic SKU optimization-focusing on best-sellers and reducing manufacturing of low-demand products to minimize waste and costs.
